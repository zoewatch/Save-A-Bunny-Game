Did you know: bunnies have one of the highest rates of euthanization?
This website aims to bring to light the fact that many bunnies are adopted for easter or are simply a used as toy and end up in the shelter.
The bunnies on this website are a rememberance for all the bunnies that never got "clicked on" and spent their last moments in a shelter.
If you can adopt or foster a bunny and are serious about long term bunny commitment or are able to donate please click on this link to help bunnies in need.
